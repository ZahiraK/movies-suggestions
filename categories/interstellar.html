JInterstellar (2014)

Action / Thriller - 2h 49m

Rating: 7.6/10

Interstellar is a mind-bending space epic directed by Christopher Nolan. The story follows Cooper, a former pilot and engineer, who is recruited for a mission to explore distant planets as Earth faces ecological collapse. Alongside a team of scientists, Cooper ventures through a wormhole to find a new home for humanity. As the journey becomes more perilous, Cooper faces time dilation, the relativity of space, and emotional struggles tied to his family back on Earth.
