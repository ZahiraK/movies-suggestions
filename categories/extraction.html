Extraction(2020)

Action/Thriller

Rating: 6.7/10 (78% liked this film)

Extraction follows Tyler Rake (Chris Hemsworth), a black-ops mercenary who embarks on a mission to rescue the kidnapped son of an international crime lord. When the mission goes awry, Tyler must navigate a dangerous criminal underworld in Dhaka, Bangladesh, while facing his own personal demons. As he fights to keep the young boy safe, Tyler is forced to confront his past and push himself to the limits of survival.
