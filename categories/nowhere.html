Nowhere (2023)

Thriller/ Drama

Rating: 6.4/10 (74% liked this film)

Nowhere tells the harrowing story of Mia, a pregnant woman who becomes trapped inside a shipping container in the middle of the sea after fleeing a war-torn country. Battling isolation, limited resources, and the uncertainty of survival, Mia fights against impossible odds to protect her unborn child and find hope in an otherwise hopeless  situation.
