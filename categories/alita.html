Alita: Battle Angel (2019)

Genre: Action, Sci-Fi, Adventurez

Rating: 7.3/10 (85% liked this film)

Alita: Battle Angel follows the story of Alita, a cyborg with no memory of her past, who is discovered and repaired by Dr. Ido in a post-apocalyptic world. As she navigates her new life in the Iron City, Alita uncovers her extraordinary combat abilities and embarks on a journey to learn the truth about her origins.
