inside(2015)

Animation/Adventure/Comedy

Rating: 8.1/10 (94% liked this film)

Plot Summary:

Inside Out takes viewers on a journey inside the mind of an 11-year-old girl named Riley, whose emotions—Joy, Sadness, Anger, Fear, and Disgust—are personified as characters in a vibrant, colorful world. When Riley faces a major life change, the emotions inside her head struggle to navigate her feelings and help her cope with the upheaval. As Joy and Sadness are accidentally sent to a distant part of the mind, they must work together to restore balance before Riley's emotional world falls apart.
