Insurgent (2015)

Genre: Action, Adventure, Sci-Fiction

Rating: 6.2/10 (80% liked this film)

Insurgent is the second installment in the Divergent series, based on the novels by Veronica Roth. The film follows Tris Prior, who is now on the run with Tobias (Four) as they try to evade the oppressive power of the government, led by the ruthless Jeanine Matthews. Tris and her allies seek to uncover the secrets hidden in a mysterious box that could hold the key to their freedom. Along the way, they face mind-bending simulations and intense physical challenges, testing their loyalty and resilience.
