Moana (2016)

Animation/Adventure/Comedy

7.6/10(86% liked this film)

Moana follows the journey of a spirited Polynesian teenager, Moana, who sets out on an epic adventure across the ocean to save her island and her people. When her island faces a mysterious curse, Moana is chosen by the ocean to restore the heart of Te Fiti, a powerful artifact that was lost long ago. Along the way, she teams up with the demigod Maui and discovers her true destiny as a leader and wayfinder.
