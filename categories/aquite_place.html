A Quiet Place (2018)

Genre: Horror, Thriller, Drama

Rating: 7.5/10 (83% liked this film)

Plot Summary:

A Quiet Place is set in a post-apocalyptic world overrun by deadly creatures that hunt using sound. The Abbott family struggles to survive by living in silence, communicating only through sign language, and carefully avoiding noise. The film intensifies when Evelyn, the mother, prepares to give birth, and the family faces unimaginable challenges as they fight to protect each other. Back to Movies
