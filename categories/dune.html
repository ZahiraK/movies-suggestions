Dune (2021)

Genre: Sci-Fi, Adventure, Drama

Rating: 8.0/10 (87% liked this film)

Dune is an epic adaptation of Frank Herbert's iconic novel, set on the desert planet of Arrakis. The story follows Paul Atreides, a young nobleman, who must navigate political intrigue and battle for control over the planet's valuable resource, the spice melange. As Paul uncovers his destiny, he confronts the harsh desert environment and the power struggles between noble houses and the Fremen, the planet's native  inhabitants.

If you're a fan of epic sci-fi, breathtaking visuals, and intricate world-building, *Dune* is a must-watch!
