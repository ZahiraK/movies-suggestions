Genre: Horror, Thriller, Mystery

Plot Summary:

Ready or Not tells the story of Grace, a newlywed who is thrust into a deadly game of hide-and-seek with her eccentric new in-laws on her wedding night. As the family’s sinister traditions come to light, Grace must fight to survive the night while uncovering their dark  secrets.
