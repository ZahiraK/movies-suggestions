7.1/10 (88% liked this film)

Plot Summary:

Elemental is set in a world where the four elements—fire, water, land, and air—are personified as characters. The story follows Ember, a fiery young woman, and Wade, a soft and calm water-based character, as they navigate the challenges of their differing elements and form an unlikely friendship. As they explore their world, they begin to question societal expectations and discover the power of embracing their differences.

Why You Should Watch

If you're a fan of imaginative animation, heartwarming storytelling, and exploring themes of connection and identity, *Elemental* is a must-watch!
