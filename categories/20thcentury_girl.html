20th Century Girl

Genre: Romance, Drama

Raiting: 7.3/10 (87% liked this film)

20th Century Girl is a heartfelt coming-of-age story set in 1999, where a teenage girl named Na Bo-ra navigates the complexities of friendship, love, and loyalty. Tasked with watching over her best friend’s crush, she becomes entangled in a sweet yet bittersweet love story of her own. The film beautifully captures the nostalgia of first love and the emotions that come with growing up.
