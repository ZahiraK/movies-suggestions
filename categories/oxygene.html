Oxygène (2021)

Sci-Fi, Thriller, Drama

raiting: 6.5/10 (78% liked this film)

Oxygène follows the story of a woman who wakes up in a cryogenic pod with no memory of how she got there. As her oxygen supply dwindles, she must piece together her fragmented memories and uncover the truth about her situation before time runs out. The confined setting and escalating tension make for a suspenseful and thought-provoking narrative.
