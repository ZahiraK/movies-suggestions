Parasite (2019)

Drama/Thriller/ Dark Comedy

Rating: 8.5/10 (94% liked this film)

Parasite tells the story of the Kim family, who live in poverty but hatch a cunning plan to infiltrate the lives of the wealthy Park family. By deceitfully securing jobs in the Park household, the Kims weave themselves into their employers' lives. However, their scheme spirals out of control when hidden secrets and unexpected events disrupt their  fragile facade.
