The Guilty (2021)

Crime, Drama, Thriller

rating: 6.3/10 (72% liked this film)

The Guilty centers on Joe Baylor, a troubled police officer reassigned to a 911 call center. During a single intense shift, Joe receives a distressing call from a kidnapped woman and becomes determined to save her. As he pieces together the mystery, his own personal struggles and moral dilemmas come to the surface, adding layers of tension and emotional depth to the story.
