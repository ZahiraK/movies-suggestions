Brave (2012)

Animation/Adventure/Fantasy

7.1/10 (78% liked this film)

Brave tells the story of Merida, a fiery and independent Scottish princess who defies tradition in her quest to shape her own destiny. When a magical mishap leads to her mother's transformation into a bear, Merida must embark on a dangerous journey to undo the curse and restore her family. Along the way, she learns valuable lessons about courage, responsibility, and the bonds between mother and daughter.
