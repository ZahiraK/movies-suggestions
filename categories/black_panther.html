black Panther(2018)

Genre: Action, Adventure, Sci-Fi

Rating: 7.3/10 (88% liked this film)

Black Panther follows T’Challa as he ascends to the throne of Wakanda, a hidden and technologically advanced African nation. His leadership is tested when a powerful adversary, Erik Killmonger, challenges his claim to the throne. As the new Black Panther, T’Challa must protect his people and their way of life while grappling with the moral dilemmas of Wakanda's isolationist policies.
