ratatouille (2007)

Animation/Comedy/ Family

8.0/10 (96% liked this film)

Ratatouille follows Remy, a talented rat with a passion for cooking, who dreams of becoming a renowned chef in Paris. Despite the challenges of being a rat in a human-dominated kitchen, Remy forms an unlikely partnership with Linguini, a young garbage boy with little cooking skill. Together, they navigate the high-stakes world of gourmet cuisine, while Remy learns valuable lessons about following his passion and embracing  his uniqueness.

Why You Should Watch

If you're a fan of heartwarming animation, creative storytelling, and the joy of pursuing your dreams, *Ratatouille* is a must-watch! Patton Oswalt voices the lovable rat Remy, whose culinary passion takes him on an unforgettable journey, serving up laughter and inspiration from start to finish.
