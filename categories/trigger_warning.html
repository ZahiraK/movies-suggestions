Trigger Warning (2022)

Drama/Thriller

Rating: 6.5/10 (73% liked this film)

Trigger Warning follows a troubled journalist, Sam, who grapples with the aftermath of a traumatic event in his life. As he attempts to uncover the truth behind a high-profile case, his mental health deteriorates, pushing him into a spiral of paranoia and emotional breakdown. The film delves into the complexities of trauma, the impact of past experiences on present life, and the fine line between reality and illusion.
