Plot Summary:

Annabelle delves into the chilling origins of the infamous cursed doll. When John and Mia Form, a young couple, are attacked by members of a satanic cult, their lives take a terrifying turn. The couple's seemingly harmless vintage doll, Annabelle, becomes a conduit for a malevolent entity that begins to wreak havoc in their home.
