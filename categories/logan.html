Logan (2017)

Action/Drama/Sci-Fi

Rating: 8.1/10 (90% liked this film)

In a future where mutants are nearly extinct, an aging Logan (Hugh Jackman), once known as Wolverine, lives in isolation and cares for an ailing Professor X (Patrick Stewart). When a young mutant girl, Laura (Dafne Keen), appears, Logan is reluctantly thrust back into a world of danger and violence. Together, they must survive a relentless pursuit by dark forces, all while Logan grapples with his own fading powers and mortality.
