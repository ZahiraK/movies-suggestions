Plot Summary:

Terrifier 3 continues the gruesome saga of Art the Clown, this time set during the Christmas season. Known for its shocking violence and grotesque creativity, the third installment promises even more horrifying and inventive kills, further solidifying Art the Clown as an iconic horror villain.
