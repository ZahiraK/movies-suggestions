John Wick (2023)

Action / Thriller - 2h 49m

Rating: 7.6/10

John Wick is a high-octane action film that redefines vengeance. After losing his wife, retired hitman John Wick (Keanu Reeves) finds solace in a puppy, a final gift from her. But when a reckless gang steals his car and kills the pup, John is forced back into a world he left behind. Fueled by rage and his lethal skills, he takes on the underworld, leaving chaos in his wake.
