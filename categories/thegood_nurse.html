The Good Nurse (2022)

Biography/ Crime/ Drama

6.9/10 (81% liked this film)

he Good Nurse is based on the chilling true story of Amy Loughren, a compassionate nurse who uncovers the dark secrets of her colleague and friend, Charles Cullen. As mysterious patient deaths rise, Amy works with authorities to expose the truth, putting her career and safety on the line. The film explores the psychological and emotional toll of her journey to stop a dangerous serial killer hidden in plain  sight.
