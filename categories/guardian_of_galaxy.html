Guardians of the Galaxy (2014)

Genre: Action, Adventure, Sci-Fictio

Rating: 8.0/10 (92% liked this film )

Guardians of the Galaxy follows a group of intergalactic misfits led by Peter Quill (Star-Lord), who come together to stop a powerful villain, Ronan the Accuser, from destroying the galaxy. The unlikely team includes Rocket, a genetically modified raccoon, Groot, a sentient tree, the assassin Gamora, and Drax the Destroyer. As they embark on their journey, they form a bond that transcends their differences.
